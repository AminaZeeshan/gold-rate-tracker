Gold rate in Pakistan

Understanding Gold Rates in Lahore Gold prices in Lahore are typically quoted in two ways: Per Tola: This is the most common unit for gold measurement in Pakistan. One tola is equal to approximately 11.66 grams. Per Gram: For smaller purchases, the price may be quoted per gram. The purity of gold is also a factor. The purest form, 24 karat , is the benchmark for pricing. Lower karat gold, like 22kt or 21kt, will be priced accordingly. Important Note: Gold prices can fluctuate throughout the day based on global market movements. The rates mentioned above may vary slightly depending on the source and the time of day you check.

Gold rate in different countries of the World

Gold prices vary from country to country due to differences in currency values, import duties, local demand, and market regulations. In the United States, gold prices closely follow the international spot rate in U.S. dollars. In countries like India, gold is often more expensive because of high import taxes and strong local demand. The United Arab Emirates, especially Dubai, offers relatively cheaper gold due to lower taxes and its position as a global trading hub. In Pakistan, rates are determined by the Karachi Sarafa Market and influenced by both the U.S. dollar exchange rate and global gold prices. These factors make gold rates different across nations, even when the gold quality is the same.
